My hobby is drawing in anime style. I love creating characters with expressive eyes, unique hairstyles, and detailed outfits inspired by Japanese animation. Drawing helps me express creativity, emotions, and storytelling through art.

It improves my patience, imagination, and sketching skills while giving me a fun way to relax. Experimenting with color palettes and poses makes each artwork exciting!

Every character I draw has a story behind it, and through my art, I can bring my imagination to life on paper. The process of sketching, inking, and coloring is therapeutic and allows me to express emotions that words sometimes cannot capture.
