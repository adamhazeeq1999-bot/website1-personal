The genius behind One Piece

Why I Admire Him

Eiichiro Oda is the creator of One Piece, one of the most successful manga in history. He started drawing at a young age and never gave up on his dream.

His storytelling, character design and world building inspire me as an artist. I love how he creates emotional moments, amazing battles and unforgettable characters. His dedication to his craft and ability to maintain consistency over decades of serialization is truly inspiring for any aspiring artist like myself.
